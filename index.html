About Me

Welcome! I am Astrologer Mohit, a dedicated practitioner with 6 years of professional experience in guiding individuals toward clarity and success through astrology.

My journey began with a deep-rooted education at a traditional Gurukul, where I cultivated a profound understanding of Vedic knowledge and spiritual practices. I further pursued my Shastri degree at the prestigious Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri National Sanskrit University, delving deeper into ancient scriptures and astrological sciences.

During my academic years, I consistently excelled in my studies, securing the 8th position in 10th grade and 10th position in 12th grade under the Uttarakhand Sanskrit Board. These accomplishments reflect my dedication to learning and excellence.

With a blend of traditional wisdom and modern interpretation, I specialize in offering precise and personalized astrological consultations tailored to your unique needs.
